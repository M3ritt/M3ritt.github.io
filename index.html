About Me

Josh Meritt, a native to Webster, NY, who is a recent graduate of SUNY Oswego and obtained a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

During the Spring semester of 2020 at SUNY Oswego, I had the experience of working with FindingFive to create an indepedent "study store". My role in this project was being the lead member of the Quality Assurance team. As the lead member, I was tasked with automating all of testing to ensure that the system was correct, reliable, time efficient and easy to understand.

Since June 2021, I have been working as an associate softare engineer at L3Harris where I specialize in automated testing development in C#. This has greatly increased my skills when it comes to working as a team, my C# knowledge as well as the intricacies of testing development and how thorough one must be when making decisions while coding. During my time here, so far, I have greatly improved the efficiency of how fast I complete a task, as well as the quality of my work as I learn from those around me.

While I am still new to the field, I consider myself to be well diversed with technical skills and am seeking to grow into the software development field. I will always welcome any opportunity to learn and grow both personally and professionally.
